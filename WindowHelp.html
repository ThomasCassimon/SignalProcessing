Introduction

Noise Suppression

Every DFT bin gathers some amount of noise. The Noise Suppression characteristic of an analysis window indicates how well an analysis window suppresses noise.
All Noise Suppression values are relative to that of the rectangular window. (So the rectangular window has a noise suppresion of 1).

In short: Lower is better.

Dynamic Range

Dynamic range indicates the ability to separate large spectral components from small (noise) components.

In short: Higher is better.
