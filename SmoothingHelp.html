Smoothing functions

Quadratic Regression

Extrema are preserved quite accurately. The smoothing is not very extreme. It smooths out the smallest peaks of the signal.

Robust Quadratic Regression

A nice middle ground between keeping the peaks in place and smoothing out a lot of the peak in the signal.

Savitzky-Golay Filter

Smooths more extreme. The peaks of the signal are reduced more than when using another function.
